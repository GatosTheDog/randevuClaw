  <instructions>
    Before submitting, audit these four touchpoints for EXACT consistency (Pitfall 1 — a single mismatch is the most common cause of rejection/1-2 week resubmission delay):
    1. Business legal name, address, and entity type as they appear in Meta Business Manager -> Business Info
    2. The same fields on the official registration/incorporation document you will upload
    3. The business name/address shown in the website footer (if a website exists for this PoC)
    4. The privacy policy URL configured in Meta Business Manager, if one is set

    Then:
    1. In Meta Business Manager, go to Business Settings -> Security Center -> Start Verification (or Business Info -> Verify your business if that entry point is shown instead).
    2. Select the legal entity type and upload the registration document, matching touchpoints 1-3 above exactly.
    3. Confirm the authorized admin/signatory listed is a real, contactable person with access to this Business Manager account.
    4. Submit.
    5. If Meta rejects with a "document mismatch" message, do not resubmit immediately — re-read the specific mismatch, fix only that field, wait 24h, then resubmit (immediate resubmission risks another rejection cycle).
  </instructions>
  <verify>
    <manual>Owner confirms in Meta Business Manager -> Business Settings -> Security Center that the verification request status is no longer "Not Started" (see acceptance_criteria)</manual>
  </verify>
