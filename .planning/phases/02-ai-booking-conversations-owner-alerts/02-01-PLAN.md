## Phase Goal

**As a** client of a Greek service business, **I want to** check availability, book, cancel, reschedule, and ask questions about my appointment entirely through natural Greek conversation on Telegram, **so that** I can manage my booking with zero friction while the owner is reliably alerted to act on it and no two clients ever end up double-booked into the same slot.

> Note: ROADMAP.md's Phase 2 `**Goal**:` line is descriptive prose, not the strict "As a / I want to / so that" format (same situation as Phase 1 — see `01-01-PLAN.md`'s identical note). This story is derived directly from that Goal plus Success Criteria 1-5, reframed for **Telegram, not WhatsApp**, per CONTEXT.md D-01/D-04 (the messaging-channel pivot is locked and project-wide, superseding ROADMAP.md's WhatsApp wording for Phase 2 implementation). No new scope is added: the owner-alert / no-double-booking guarantee (Success Criterion 5) is folded into the "so that" clause as the reliability condition the client-facing story depends on; OWNR-02 (owner accept/reject) is realized across Plans 02-04/02-05.
