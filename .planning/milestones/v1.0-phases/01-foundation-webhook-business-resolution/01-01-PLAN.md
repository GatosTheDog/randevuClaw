## Phase Goal

**As a** client of a Greek service business, **I want to** text a business-specific WhatsApp deep link and be reliably routed to the right business, with a data-consent notice on my first contact, **so that** I can start a booking conversation with zero confusion about who I'm talking to, even if my message gets sent or delivered twice.

> Note: ROADMAP.md's Phase 1 `**Goal**:` line is descriptive prose, not the strict "As a / I want to / so that" format. This story is derived directly from that Goal plus Success Criteria 1-4 (business resolution, consent notice, dedup, not-found handling) without adding new scope. Success Criterion 5 (Meta Business Verification) is an operational prerequisite, not part of the client-facing story — it is covered by Plan 01-04.

<objective>
Stand up the RandevuClaw project scaffold, the Drizzle/Postgres schema (businesses, messages, client_business_relationships), and the two fixture businesses that every later Phase 1 plan depends on. This is the substrate layer of the Walking Skeleton (see SKELETON.md) — no webhook or WhatsApp logic yet, just a working, tested, deployable-shaped Node/TS project with a live schema pushed to Neon and two seeded businesses provable by query.

Purpose: Every subsequent plan (webhook handler, dedup, consent) imports `config`, `logger`, `db`, `schema`, and `queries` from this plan. Getting the contract right here (exact table/column names, exact query function signatures) means Plans 01-02 and 01-03 need zero rework.
Output: Compiling TypeScript project, Jest test runner working, live Neon schema matching schema.ts, two fixture businesses queryable by slug.
</objective>
